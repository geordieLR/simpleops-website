How SimpleOps handles information you provide.

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Information we collect

We may collect your name, business name, contact details and information you choose to provide through an enquiry, consultation or service engagement.

How we use it

We use this information to respond to enquiries, provide agreed services, maintain business records and improve SimpleOps. We do not sell personal information.

Service information

Information supplied during a Business Tune-Up or Cost & Spend Review is treated as confidential and used only for the agreed work, subject to the service agreement.

Storage and providers

We may use reputable hosting, email, form-processing and business software providers. Information may be processed outside New Zealand where those providers operate.

Your rights

You may ask to access or correct personal information we hold about you. Contact hello@simpleops.co.nz.

Important

This is a practical launch policy and should be reviewed by a New Zealand privacy professional before collecting sensitive customer or employee information.
