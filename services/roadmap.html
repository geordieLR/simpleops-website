When a Roadmap is useful

Change is coming.

  • The business is preparing to grow.
  • The owner wants less day-to-day dependency on them.
  • Succession or leadership change is ahead.
  • A new location, service, channel or market is being considered.
  • A major system or investment needs to be justified.
  • Several improvements are competing for attention.

What it clarifies

What happens first—and why.

Priorities: what matters now, what can wait and what should not be done.

Ownership: who needs to own which decisions and responsibilities.

Information: what the business needs to see and trust.

Investment: where time, money or technology may be justified.
