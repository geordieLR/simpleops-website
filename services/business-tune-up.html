What’s included

1. A short intake discussion

We begin with the business context, what has changed and what you would most value understanding.

2. Customer-eye review

We look at the business as a prospective customer would—how easy it is to understand, trust and contact.

3. Up to 10 hours inside the business

Over one working week, we talk with employees and observe how work, information and customer needs actually move through the business.

4. Findings and recommendations

You receive a concise findings presentation, prioritised opportunities, quick wins and clear next-step recommendations.

What we look for

  • Repeated manual tasks and unnecessary checks.
  • Waiting, bottlenecks and unclear handovers.
  • Workarounds that have become the normal process.
  • Information that is difficult to find or trust.
  • Tasks that depend heavily on memory or one experienced person.
  • Practical improvements that could create capacity, control or consistency.

What this is—and is not

The Tune-Up is designed to understand the work, not judge the people.

  • Not an employee performance review.

  • Not a generic productivity audit.
  • Not a theoretical consulting report.
  • Not a commitment to larger work.
